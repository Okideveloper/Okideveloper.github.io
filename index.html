My journey into Web Development

Being a military spouse involves traveling and moving home to home every 3-4 years, which was interfering with my career achievements. I needed a steady job that would follow me during our next duty station move. I started thinking, “what should I do with my life?” and then searched and took all the personality and career quizzes I could find, haha… To my surprise, most revealing Web Development. I already have an Associates Degree in Information technology and a passion for fixing computer problems. Hey! I guess the online career quizzes do work because, here you are reading how I discovered my career! Now my job allows me to work remotely during PSC season while continuing my love for coding. I hope you enjoyed my story. If you need assistance with websites, I am happy to listen to your ideas.

I’m sure we can create something amazing!
